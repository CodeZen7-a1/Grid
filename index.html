Beauty Of Rajdhani Satara

Kas Pathar

Located 25 km from Satara, Kas Pathar is a valley of flowers, known for its seasonal blooming meadows and unique biodiversity.

Jarandeshwar

Jarandeshwar Hill is near Satara city, famous for its trekking trail and a temple at the summit offering scenic views.

Mahuli

Located in Satara district, Mahuli is a peaceful village known for its natural surroundings and cultural heritage.

Mahabaleshwar

Located about 45 km from Satara, Mahabaleshwar is a popular hill station known for strawberries, viewpoints, and cool climate.

Vasota Fort

Vasota is a jungle fort located in the Koyna Wildlife Sanctuary, about 60 km from Satara, ideal for adventurous treks.

Thoseghar Waterfall

Situated around 20 km from Satara, Thoseghar is famous for its tall and majestic waterfalls surrounded by greenery.

Ajinkyatara Fort

Located in the heart of Satara city, Ajinkyatara Fort offers panoramic views and is a symbol of Maratha pride and resilience.

Pratapgad Fort

Situated about 24 km from Mahabaleshwar, Pratapgad is a historic fort where Shivaji Maharaj defeated Afzal Khan in 1659.
